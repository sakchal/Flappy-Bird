Flappy Bird
    
How To Play:
    1. To start the game, press the 'ENTER' key 
    2. After starting the game, you will have 10 seconds to move the bird to the tunnel.
    3. If you hit the enemy, bird then you LOSE!
    3. If you get the bird to the tunnel within 10 seconds, you WIN! 
       If you go over 10 seconds, you LOSE!
    
To move the bird, use the 'ARROW KEYS':
    - 'RIGHT' arrow: moves the bird to the right
    - 'LEFT' arrow: moves the bird to the left
    - 'UP' arrow: moves the bird up
    - 'DOWN' arrow: moves the bird down
